What does this mod?

You can find spraycans in some containers:
- 9 colours (black, white, red, blue, green, yellow, orange, violet, cyan)
- 18 shapes to spray (arrows, simple icons, special icons)
- Single and Multiplayer support!
- Language support: german, polish
